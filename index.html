Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

In short: Naksh is designed privacy-first. Your birth data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data. The app works fully offline after initial setup.

1. Information We Collect

Birth Data (required for core functionality):

  • Date of birth
  • Time of birth (optional - approximate time accepted)
  • Place of birth (city name only)

This data is used solely to calculate your astronomical birth chart using the Swiss Ephemeris (NASA JPL DE431 data). It is stored encrypted on your device using iOS Keychain or Android EncryptedSharedPreferences.

Account Data (optional):

  • If you sign in via Google, Apple, or Facebook SSO, we receive your name and email address from the identity provider.
  • Sign-in is optional. The app works fully without an account.

Usage Data:

  • We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics (e.g., which features are used most) to improve the app.
  • We do not track individual behavior or build user profiles.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Birth data: To compute your Vedic birth chart, daily energy guidance, planetary transits, and compatibility scores. All calculations happen locally on your device.
  • Account data: To enable cloud backup of your preferences (if you opt in) and to manage your subscription.
  • Usage analytics: To understand which features are valuable and to fix bugs.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local-first: Birth data is encrypted using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences).
  • Cloud sync (optional): If you sign in, your preferences may be backed up to Firebase (Google Cloud). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • No plain-text storage: Sensitive data is never stored in plain text or in URLs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, cloud database (optional), and serverless functions. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. Processes purchase data only. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API: Generates personalized interpretations. We send anonymized chart data (planetary positions only, no personally identifiable information) to generate daily guidance text.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app (Profile tab).
  • Delete: Use "Reset Data" in Profile to permanently erase all local data. If signed in, use "Delete Account" to remove all cloud data.
  • Portability: Your birth data can be viewed and manually recorded at any time.
  • Opt out: You can use the app without creating an account or enabling cloud features.

6. Children's Privacy

Naksh is not directed at children under 18. We require age verification during onboarding. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Naksh is a native mobile app and does not use browser cookies. We do not use advertising trackers. The Facebook SDK is included solely for Facebook Login functionality - we do not use it for ad tracking or audience building.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
